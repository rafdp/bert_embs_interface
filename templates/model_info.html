Model info

Kaggle
The model consists of a small BERT layer (12 attention heads, dimension 768)
with fine-tuning on the pooling layer, and two fully-connected layers (dimensions 512 and 256)
plus a softmax activation layer for each target.
The model is built on Keras with Tensorflow backend, runs on a single GTX1080.
After extensive filtering, the dataset consists of 350k entries, training takes about 1.5 hours.
A modular architecture is used, which helps in trying out different models by adding folders with a Model class.
